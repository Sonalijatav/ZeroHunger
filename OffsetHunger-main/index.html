Our Request

No one should have to worry about whether they have food on their plate or a roof over their heads. But the reality is that there are far too many people who are in this exact situation. And we want to do something about it. We cook our own food as well as collect leftovers which are still fresh from food companies and deliver them to the homeless population in and around various districts. As noble as this cause is, we cannot do this ourselves and thats why we require your humble donations no matter how small. Because every single penny that is being donated is used to provide high quality healthy food to the lesser privileged people.
